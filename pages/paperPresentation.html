It’s time to show who’s the boss when it comes to the command over the subject. The staple diet of all technical events is undoubtedly the presentation event, yet it has something in it which makes it exciting. If you have an idea which has been taking shelter in your mind that would change lifestyles visibly, make inroads into the development of geeky technology or even make some known technology effective and efficient then we offer you the stage to show who you are to the world.
Authors: Maximum 4.
Presenters: 2 members.
Rules:
1. No restrictions on number of authors per paper but maximum of two can present it.
2. Participants have to submit their FULL paper for scrutiny, abstracts WILL NOT be entertained.
3. The paper should be in IEEE format not exceeding 6 pages.
4. The document format has to be in Printable Document Format(.pdf) only and file name should be saved with the respective title.
5. Soft copy of the full paper should be mailed to sit.technisium2018@gmail.com with the domain name as the subject.
6. Name of the participants, college name, email address and contact numbers should be mentioned in the mail.
7. Shortlisted participants will be intimated through email/SMS.
8. Three hard copies and one soft copy of the paper should be brought on the day of presentation.
9. Presentation time + Queries time = 10min + 2min = 12min.
The rules may be slightly modified during the competition. The organizers reserve the right for the same.
