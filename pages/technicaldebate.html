INVICTUS


In today’s world, an engineer needs to be up to date with the latest technologies of the world. It’s time to show who’s the boss when it comes to the command over the Subject.

RULES AND REGULATIONS

● Team of 2 members.

Round 1:

Conventional Format:
● 1 member of the team will speak in favour of the topic & the other member will counter his/her teammate’s arguments, thus speaking against the topic.
● Every speaker will have 5 minutes 20 seconds to complete the speech.
● After both the speeches, the team will face cross-questions by both judges & the audience.

Round 2:

Parliamentary Format:
● 2 teams will debate against each other on a common topic.
● 1 team will speak in favour of the topic & the other team will counter their arguments, thus speak against the topic.
● The topic will be released on the spot & the teams will get 30 minutes to prepare their arguments.
● Every speaker will have 5 minutes 20 seconds to complete the speech.
● The speaker positions are as follows:
○ For: Speaker 1
○ Against: Speaker 1
○ For: Speaker 2
○ Against: Speaker 2
● During a speech, the opposing team members can take a maximum of 2 POIs.
● POIs are prohibited during the first & the last minute of the speech. There should be a gap of atleast 15 seconds between the 2 POIs.
