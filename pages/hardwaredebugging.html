DEBUGGER


A major part of engineering consists of problem solving and by this event, we test just that in the participants. The participants will be given a circuit with a minor fault and they will have to debug the problem and make the circuit work. Analyzing networks and putting theoretical knowledge into a practical scenario is the main focus of this event.
This event of debugging hardware consists of two rounds.
First round is written test, which is based on electronic circuits.
The shortlisted participants for final round will be given circuits with bugs on some simulation software’s and need to debug the same.
1. Max 2 in a team.
2. First round is of duration 45 mins.
3. Second round duration is 60 mins.
4. No provision for resources like internet, calculator, books, mobile phones.
