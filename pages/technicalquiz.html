QUIZ MANIA


In today’s world, an engineer needs to be up to date with the latest technologies of the world. And to test just that, we have a Tech Quiz, which will test the knowledge base of the participants and urge them to extend it to newer horizons. A preliminary round will be held to choose the best teams who will be put up against each other in a Quiz Show style rounds complete with our very own Quiz Masters.

Rules:

1. Maximum of two participants per team.
2. The event will be held in two rounds: a preliminary round followed by the finals.
3. In the preliminary round the participants will be given a written test, the duration of which will be 30 minutes.
4. The teams scoring above the cut-off will be getting through to the second round.
5. General rules of quizzing apply.
6. Decision of the judges/coordinators is final.
